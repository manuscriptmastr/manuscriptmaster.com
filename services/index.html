Teaching

In addition to being a prolific pianist and composer, Mr. Martin has a passion for teaching the younger generation to excel in fine performance and writing. He currently offers instruction in solo piano, composition, theory, aural skills, and Finale software. He is available for various educational venues, including:

  • Private lessons
  • Regular instruction in private school/homeschool establishments
  • Masterclasses
  • Guest lectures

Whether your student or class is just beginning to play Middle C, write songs, or use Finale software, or seeking to refine phrasing, technique, or artistry, Joshua can ignite the innovative spark in your pupil and communicate effectively both the fundamentals and subtleties of extraordinary music making.

To bring Mr. Martin to your school, receive information regarding private lessons (including studio policy, teaching philosophy, and tuition), or submit any other educational inquiry, email Joshua at me@manuscriptmastr.com.

Performance

Do you need a pianist/accompanist for your concert, recital, wedding, business meeting, reception, or service? Contact Joshua for gigs featuring live performance of classical, contemporary, or jazz genres. A tuned acoustic piano is required. Email for more details. A notice of at least 4 weeks is preferred but not required for submission.
